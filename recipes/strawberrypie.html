Strawberry pie recipe

Tasty strawberry pie

This is my favourite strawberry pie, and it's very easy to prepare. The recipe was gotten many years ago from the Sussex County Strawberry Farm in North Jersey

Ingredients

  • 1 quart fresh strawberrries, hulled
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Arrange 1/2 of strawberries in baked pastery shell. Place remaining SB in a medium saucepan
  3. Add sugar to the SB in the saucepan; place over medium heat and bring to a boil, stirring frequently
  4. Whisk cornstarch and water together in a bowl. Stir cornstarch mixture gradually into boiling strawberry mixture. Reduce heat and simmer mixture until thickened, about 10 minutes stirring constantly
  5. Pour cooked strawberry mixture over berries in pastry shell. Refrigerate until chilled and set, at least 3 hours.
  6. Before serving, whip cream in a medium bowl until soft peaks form.
  7. Serve each slice of pie with a dollop of whipped cream. Enjoy!

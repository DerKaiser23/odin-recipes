LASAGNA RECIPE

Easy lasagna

Making perfect lasagna doesn't have to be tedious. This highly rated recipe comes together quickly with a short ingredient list

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghettti sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghettti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the parmesan, and seasonings
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
